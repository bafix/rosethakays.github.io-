About

Bridging Technology & Community

Accomplished academic and researcher with a PhD in Computer Science and over two decades of combined teaching and industry experience in Namibia and beyond.

Her research focuses on Software Development, Human–Computer Interaction (HCI), and the design of technologies that support educational resilience through human-centred and participatory approaches with marginalised communities — particularly indigenous San learners and Namibian youth.

Since 2015 she has been lecturing at NUST, mentoring students, supervising research projects, and contributing to curriculum development and community-focused educational initiatives. She is also an active conference organiser and workshop facilitator on the African continent.

Deeply committed to ethical practice in an evolving digital landscape, she believes technology should serve people — not the other way around.
